Carpaccio alla Pizzaiola

Wash the tomatoes and remove the seeds; cube the tomatoes then

dress with oil, salt, pepper, lemon juice, and oregano....

h2>LIST OF INGREDIENTS
  • 3/4 LB. of beef carpaccio
  • 12 WHOLE of Taggiasca olives, pitted
  • 12 WHOLE of caper berries, rinsed
  • 8 WHOLE of ripe plum tomatoes
  • Lemon
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • olive oil

Directions

  1. Wash the tomatoes and remove the seeds; cube the tomatoes then dress with oil, salt, pepper, lemon juice, and oregano.
  2. Place the carpaccio onto plates, dress with a bit of oil and season with salt and pepper; top with the cubed tomatoes, olives, and caper berries.
